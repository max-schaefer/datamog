<div>

Pokémon have <span class="pk pk--name">ID</span>, <span class="pk pk--name">name</span>, <span class="pk pk--types">types</span>, <span class="pk pk--abil">abilities</span> and <span class="pk pk--stats">stats</span>, and can perform moves, specified in the Pokédex ([English](https://www.pokemon.com/us/pokedex), [中文](https://tw.portal-pokemon.com/play/pokedex)).

Each player has a team of Pokémon.
They use moves to reduce the opponent's HPs ("hitpoints") to zero.
Rules govern when each player may move, and how much damage each move does.
