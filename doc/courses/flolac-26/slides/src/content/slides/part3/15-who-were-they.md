**Youngest: Assad Thomas**

Five months old, in third class.
Separated from his mother as the ship sank, he was carried to a lifeboat by the stewardess Violet Jessop, and the two were reunited aboard the *Carpathia*.

Born 1911; died of pneumonia in 1931.

First class; floated away on a wooden plank, then climbed onto a capsized (upside-down) boat.
Born 1864, died 1945.
